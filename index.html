About

The ILITHA Youth Development Programme is a programme that was established in 2019 October by a group of incredible, talented and determined young people who aim to develop the youth in their community. This programme is situated in Qutsa one of the rural areas of Cofimvaba/ Tsomo in Chris Hani district under Intsika Yethu municipality in ward 7. As the ILitha Youth Development Programme we seek to provide great opportunities for young people, create a healthy and safe environment for our own community. We seek to develop disciplined, talented, educated, informed, responsible and active young people in our society. It is in our heart to see that talents and capabilities are discovered and placed into consideration in the process of our development. Our aim is to make a united, crime free and substance abuse free community. The founders of this programme are the youth of Qutsa location who are university students and those who have matriculated. We currently have Yonela Toyisi, Sinazo Mpotulo, Linda Sijula, Asenako Ngamlana, Bukhulu Ngwenya, Sisonke Stemele, Silindokuhle Toyisi and Litha Xego. They have already invested their time in this programme sharing ideas and planning in order for the latter to succeed. Programmes like sports, tutoring, arts and other programmes are about to be implemented to develop and search youth talent and teach them discipline so as to groom creative active young people. The ILitha Youth Development Programme is a new programme that seeks to embrace and promote the importance of culture and originality for a better future. Programme background The Community Our Community is faced with a high unemployment rate, increasing number of drop- outs, teenage pregnancy, drug and alcohol abuse. The above factors together with the increasing crime rate are highly influenced by lack of information and motivation. The violence committed by young people against each other is becoming out of hand for that reason we believe that the activities that come with this programme will work in sink to try and solve this issue. The driving force of ILitha is to do something productive to save our beloved brothers and sisters from this detrimental behaviour while we assist to shape their way to success. The community participation will be of significance as this programme will be a second home of children their children, the voice for voiceless and platform to showcase the hidden talents of our community. We aim to give hope, second chances and empower each other to have a brighter future. In our community there was never an opportunity like this programme which will be focusing on youth development. Vision

As this is the one and only youth programme around the ward 7 of Intsika Yethu municipality we would like to see this programme very successful, influential and fully equipped to development the current and future youth generations. It is our vision to see the ILitha Youth Development Programme being local and provincial recognised for rural youth development in the province of the Eastern Cape and be a leading example of rural youth development in surrounding communities and beyond.

Mission

It is our mission to provide access to information to provide opportunities for rural youth to have access to the information as equally like those who are in urban areas. We will focus on the development of young people, promoting unity among the community and we will assist our youth to achieve more on their academics and in life in general. This is to see that our community has a the better future.
